THE STORY

A 10-Year-Old Who Refused to Wait

My name is Oliver. I'm 10 years old, I live in California, and I have Tourette syndrome.

Most people think kids have to wait until they grow up to build something real. I didn't wait.

I built KidRising — a social network for kids. Then a parliament where kids vote on real laws. Then a country called KidRising Nation with its own passports, constitution, and national anthem. Then a platform connecting all of it together. Then an arcade shooter called HYPERBLAST ULTRA. Then a music career — my debut single is called "I Feel You", about understanding what it feels like to have a disability, because I do.

"I Feel You" — Oliver's debut single. About the moment he told a friend with a disability: "I understand. Because I have one too."

And now: KidForever Cards. A trading card game I designed from scratch — 20 characters, 4 rarities, 8 worlds, battle mechanics, and pack opening animations. I built the whole digital version myself.

But I want it to be real. Not printed on my dad's printer. Real foil cards, real booster packs, real packaging — so every kid who opens one feels the same thing I felt opening my first Pokémon pack.

This Kickstarter will pay for professional card printing. If we hit $5,000, every backer gets their cards. If we go further — we add more characters, more worlds, and maybe a real tournament.

I'm 10. I built all of this before I turned 11. Help me make the cards real.

Questions

Who is Oliver?
Oliver is a 10-year-old from California with Tourette syndrome. He built KidRising (a social network), KidRising Parliament (a kid government), KidRising Nation (a country with real passports and a constitution), HYPERBLAST ULTRA (an arcade game), a music career, and now KidForever Cards — all before turning 11.
Is the game already built?
Yes! The digital version of KidForever Cards is live right now at oliveryvo-cmd.github.io/kidforever-cards/. You can open packs, battle, and collect today. This Kickstarter is to make the physical version — real printed cards.
When will cards ship?
If we hit our goal, cards ship within 60 days. We've already contacted card printers and have quotes ready. No delays — Oliver is serious about this.
What if you don't hit $5,000?
Kickstarter is all-or-nothing. If we don't hit $5,000, you pay nothing and we don't print. But we believe we will — and so should you.
What happens if you raise MORE than $5,000?
Stretch goals: $10,000 = add 10 new cards. $20,000 = full tournament kit. $50,000 = Oliver goes to a real trading card convention to debut KidForever Cards to the world.
Can I get a custom card if I'm outside the US?
Custom cards ($100 tier) ship worldwide. Booster packs currently ship US only, but message us if you're international and we'll figure it out.
