01 — THE CONCEPT
SERIES OVERVIEW

What Is KidForever?

KidForever is a 26-episode animated adventure series for kids ages 6–14. Equal parts action, heart, and humor — think Avatar: The Last Airbender meets Pokémon, with a hero who has a real disability and wins anyway.

The show follows OLIVER — a 10-year-old with Tourette syndrome who discovers that the characters he invented in his bedroom are real. They live in a secret multiverse called the KidForever Universe, made up of eight distinct worlds — and they've been waiting for him to lead them.

But The Boss — an ancient dragon warlord — is tearing the worlds apart. Oliver must travel between worlds, collect allies, and use the one power nobody expected: the ability to believe in things nobody else will.

"The greatest animated series aren't about superpowers. They're about a kid who refuses to give up. KidForever is that story — and it happens to be true."
02 — THE CREATOR
WHO MADE THIS

Built by a Kid.
For Every Kid.

OLIVER
CREATOR · SHOWRUNNER · AGE 10
Oliver is a 10-year-old from California with Tourette syndrome. Before age 11 he built KidRising — a social network for kids — a parliament where kids vote on real laws, a country called KidRising Nation with real passports and a constitution, an arcade shooter, a trading card game with 20 original characters, and released his debut single "I Feel You."

KidForever is not a pitch from a Hollywood writer. It is a universe one kid built alone, from his bedroom, because he needed a world where kids like him were the heroes.

Oliver's story is the show's greatest asset. No animated series in history has been created by a 10-year-old with a disability. The press writes itself. The mission is real. The audience already exists — every kid who has ever been told they can't.

05 — EPISODES
SEASON ONE · 13 EPISODES

Season One:
"The Chosen Kid"

Season One follows Oliver discovering the KidForever universe exists, recruiting his first five allies, and facing The Boss for the first time — ending on a cliffhanger that sets up a 3-season arc.

EPISODE 01
"The Kid Who Built a Universe"
Oliver is just a regular kid with Tourette syndrome who draws characters in his notebook. One night, a lightning bolt hits his desk — and Puffblaze crawls out of the page. The KidForever universe is real. And it needs him.
EPISODE 02
"Welcome to HYPERBLAST"
Oliver and Puffblaze arrive in HYPERBLAST — a world at war. They must survive long enough to find Hyperbot, who holds the key to reaching the other worlds.
EPISODE 03
"I Feel You"
Oliver meets Luna in FluffLand. She's the fastest being alive — and she's been running from something her whole life. Oliver tells her: "I understand what it feels like. Because I have one too." She joins the team.
EPISODE 07
"The Boss Was a Kid Once"
A flashback episode reveals The Boss's origin. He was a kid who wanted to build something great — and nobody believed in him. This is the episode that makes the villain human.
EPISODE 13
"KidForever" (Season Finale)
The five heroes face The Boss for the first time. They don't win. But Oliver does something nobody expected — he offers The Boss a seat at the table. End of Season One. The universe holds. For now.
